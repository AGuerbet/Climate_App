Climate Trends Explorer

This interface explores long-term climatic changes using standardized indicators derived from reanalysis data. Thermal regime captures average conditions and temperature extremes, while hydrological stress reflects total rainfall, drought intensity (SPI), and the contribution of extreme precipitation events. Additional indicators quantify changes in cloud cover and incoming solar radiation. Each variable is computed annually and compared against a 1980–2024 baseline to detect directional trends over time.
